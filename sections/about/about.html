Ford Mustang
our company

In 2007, following the introduction of the fifth generation Ford Mustang, the Shelby name was returned for new high-performance versions of the Mustang. It is presented in two versions - GT500 and from 2015 GT350. The GT350 comes with a naturally aspirated engine and the GT500 with a mechanical supercharger [4]. There is also a version adapted for the GT350R track. The 2011 Shelby GT500 weighs approximately 1,735 kg.
